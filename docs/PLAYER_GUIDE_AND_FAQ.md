# Ashveil Player Guide & FAQ

## 1) Core Combat Rule

- Roll a `d20`, add your action modifier, compare to a target number.
- If your total meets or beats the target, it succeeds.
- Natural `20` on d20 is a critical success.
- Natural `1` on d20 is a critical failure.

## 2) AC vs DC (Simple)

- **AC (Armor Class):** used for attack actions against enemies.
  - Example: `12 vs AC 14` -> miss.
- **DC (Difficulty Class):** used for checks/spells/challenges.
  - Example: `12 vs DC 10` -> success.

## 3) What Your Stats Do

- **STR:** physical power, heavy melee actions.
- **DEX:** agility, finesse attacks, movement, evasive play.
- **CON:** toughness, defensive checks, survivability.
- **INT:** learned magic/analysis style actions.
- **WIS:** perception, scouting, intuition, healing flavor.
- **CHA:** persuasion, leadership, social pressure.

Modifier rule: `(stat - 10) / 2`, rounded down.

## 4) Enemy Intel (Party-Shared)

- Enemy HP/AC/weak points are tracked in real game state.
- Players do **not** always see exact values immediately.
- Use inspect/scout style actions to reveal enemy details.
- Discovery is party-shared: once discovered, all party members can use it.

Reveal levels:
- `none`: narrative clues only.
- `partial`: some combat intel.
- `full`: exact HP, AC, weak points, and kit.

## 5) Turn Flow

1. Player submits action.
2. Roll(s) happen if needed.
3. Result and state effects are applied.
4. Narration describes the outcome.
5. Next turn starts.

## 6) Quick Play Advice

- High STR/CON builds should frontline and pressure enemies.
- High DEX builds should scout, flank, and avoid risky face-tanking.
- Casters should manage mana and target openings, not spam.
- If enemy stats are hidden, prioritize one scouting action early.
- Track condition effects and action legality hints in feed text.

## FAQ

### Why did my attack fail?
You likely rolled below target AC after modifier, or hit a critical failure.

### Why did my spell fail?
You likely rolled below DC, or had a critical failure.

### Why is mana consumed on failure?
Casting still consumes effort/resources even if the effect misses.

### Why can an action be blocked?
Some actions are marked illegal by rules/context and won’t resolve mechanically.

### Why did numbers appear later for an enemy?
Enemy data can be hidden until your party successfully scouts/inspects.
